Walk in nature

In order to do this task, I chose a route in the outskirts of the town where I live, Tona. The first time I went this route in the afternoon, I saw darker colours in the end of the route because the dusk was coming by, but I also observed orange, yellow and dark blue colours in the sky as it was clear.

Nevertheless, the second time I went to this route it was cloudy so when I arrived at the starting point, it started raining and I decided to not count this time because I did not have any picture or drawing of that day. The third day I went to the route, it was in the morning, but it was cloudy and I could also observe how the season was changing because flowers sprouted, although far away, I could observe snow on the mountains.

I loved this route because I was alone all the time, I did not see anyone, so I was able to experience peace of mind and connect with nature.
